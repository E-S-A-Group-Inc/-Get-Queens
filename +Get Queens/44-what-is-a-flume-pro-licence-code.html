What is a Flume Pro licence code?

Direct Version

Access to Flume Pro features are unlocked through a Flume Pro licence code, which can be purchased through our website.

  • Flume Pro licence codes do not expire. When you purchase a Flume Pro upgrade, the licence is issued in your name and your email address. Please ensure these are correct, as you will not be able to recover your licence code in the future without contacting us.
  • To unlock Flume Pro features, you must activate your licence code for each Mac you own (up to a maximum of 5 Macs). Instructions on how to do this are included in your Flume Pro licence code email, as well as in the help documentation.
  • If you purchased Flume Pro as an in-app purchase from the Mac App Store, this is not your licence code. You must convert and follow the instructions to migrate your purchase from Apple, in order to receive a Flume Pro licence code.
  • Flume Pro licence codes are not tied to or associated with your Instagram account. While Flume Pro unlocks the ability to add more than one account to Flume, this doesn't tie your licence with a specific Instagram account.


Setapp Version

If you use Flume for Mac via Setapp, this version already includes Flume Pro's features, and no upgrade is necessary. Your Setapp monthly subscription covers the cost of Flume Pro.

  • If you are on a paid plan, you can use your Setapp subscription on multiple Macs.
  • If you cancel your Setapp subscription, you will be unable to launch Flume once your billing cycle ends.

For all other Setapp related questions, please refer to Setapp's support website.
